Ooty

Ooty officially known as Udagamandalam (also known as Ootacamund About this soundlisten (helpÂ·info); abbreviated as Udhagai), is a town and a municipality in the Niligiris District of the Indian state of Tamil Nadu. It is located 86 km north of Coimbatore and 128 km south of Mysore and is the headquarters of the Nilgiris district. It is a popular hill station located in the Nilgiri Hills. Originally occupied by the Toda people, the area came under the rule of the East India Company at the end of the 18th century.
